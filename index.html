No. The chassis uses a tuned front‑to‑back path and a quiet bottom intake. Components are selected and profiled per model to maintain sustained performance within acoustic targets.

Founder Edition batches ship in 6–8 weeks. You’ll receive a concierge chat the week before delivery for setup preferences.
